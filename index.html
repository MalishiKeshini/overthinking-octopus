Thought Journal

    Mindfulness Exercises

    Choose from the following exercises:

    1. Breathing Exercise: Take slow, deep breaths for 5 minutes.
    2. Body Scan: Close your eyes and focus on each part of your body, starting from your toes and moving up to your head.
    3. Gratitude Practice: Write down three things you're grateful for.

    Tips to Manage Overthinking

    • Recognize the pattern of overthinking.
    • Challenge your thoughts with realistic perspectives.
    • Set time limits for worrying.
    • Take action, even if it's a small step.
    • Practice mindfulness and meditation.
    • Seek support from a therapist or counselor.
